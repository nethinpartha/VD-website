  1. Get closer to consumers: Create compelling value that strengthens your brand and attracts and keeps customers
  2. Improve collaboration through value chain: Create synergies, and reduce waste and inefficiencies by sharing data and insights
  3. Improve visibility: Embed insights across organization that leads to better understanding
  4. Strengthen decision-making: Advance to a more predictive approach to decision making
