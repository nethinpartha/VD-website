Ensuring businesses are aligned with their objectives and customer requirements

VueData provides service management support to ensure your business maximizes revenue while minimizing downtime. Our strategists, architects, developers, and engineers have world-class skills in operations, process design, and large-scale project delivery.

Service management is a set of workflows and technologies that enables development, delivery, and management of IT related services to be as efficient as possible. It aims to increase productivity, lower costs, and improve end-user satisfaction.

Our team has extensive experience in creating the strategy and plan for deployment, provide full scale deployment expertise and change management strategies that increase adoption of IT Operations management (ITOM), IT Service Management (ITSM), IT Business Management (ITBM), IT Asset Management (ITAM) and Human Resource Service Management (HRSM) modules.

Some of the services we deliver include:

  1. Assessment of the current IT capabilities, services and processes
  2. Audit and control for operations, security and governance practices to meet government and compliance requirements
  3. Roadmap development & planning to accomplish your goals and objectives
  4. Design, improve, implement and transform IT services and processes around international standards, best practices, and frameworks
  5. Develop and deploy Disaster Recovery Plans and Business Continuity Plans
  6. Build on Agile practices and continual improvement to meet customer requirements and effectively respond to the changing environment
