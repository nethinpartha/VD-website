Internet of Things

Our IoT practice team built expertise on developing The Internet of Things (IoT) and Machine to Machine (M2M) applications, transforming bits of information from sensors, chips, cameras, and any network of physical objects accessed through the internet to actionable information driving business values.

We work closely with our clients to understand business requirements and help discover the competitive advantage that IoT solutions can bring to their organization. We then apply IoT solution to transform and implement the business processes with our group of expert IoT consultants.

Our IoT Services offering includes -

  • Architecture and Design IoT application to implement business features supporting online and offline mode as business needs.
  • Integrate circuit boards like Raspberry PI, Arduino with external devices like sensors, cameras, hard drives to capture and process information right at the source.
  • Back-end infrastructure support to allow device provisioning, security, account management based on product needs.
  • Design and Development of user interface, web and mobile app user experience. Deliver reports, alerts and messages on different target systems based on configuration.
  • Develop package to support remote software upgrades, and solution packages deployment to devices.
  • Cloud integration to archive captured data into AWS and Azure, and execute business processes from cloud to transform bits of information into business values.
