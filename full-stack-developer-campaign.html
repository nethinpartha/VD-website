Lead fullstack developer

India – Chennai

As a Full stack lead, you will take on a pivotal role in our organization, guiding and mentoring a team of talented developers while setting a compelling vision for our projects. You will collaborate closely with stakeholders, both onsite and with clients, to understand their requirements, provide expert guidance, and ensure successful project delivery. Your strong leadership skills will empower the team to excel, while your technical expertise in full stack development will drive the creation of innovative and impactful solutions that exceed client expectations.

Required skills

  • 6+ years of proven experience as a Full Stack Developer or similar role
  • Strong Knowledge of multiple back-end languages (e.g. Python, Node.js) and JavaScript frameworks (React.js)
  • Familiarity with Kubernetes architecture
  • Experience in CI/CD tools like Jenkins
  • Should have experience in multiple payment system integration like Stripe, Paypal, RevenueCat
  • Should have strong knowledge in Bitbucket, GitHub, GitLab
  • Strong Knowledge in databases (MySQL, MongoDB), web servers (e.g Apache) and UI/UX design
  • Familiarity with Disaster recovery and Business continuity process

Responsibilities
