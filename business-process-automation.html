Intelligent business process automation using AI

Automate repetitive tasks using AI powered bots to increase productivity and ROI.

As a customer-focused company, we understand that providing exceptional support is critical to our clients' success. To ensure that their customers receive the best possible support, we understand their pain points in handling repetitive tasks and offer advanced technologies like RPA (Robotic Process Automation) + AI.

Businesses have many processes or repetitive tasks that are carried out manually today. Considering the nature of these tasks, it is monotonous for the employees and error prone. The amount of workforce a company must invest in such processes or tasks are directly proportional to the volume of data or the number of tasks that needs to be processed.With Robotic Process Automation, we can automate these repetitive tasks or processes which will increase the productivity, ROI, and the quality. Our experts can understand the business processes well and know how to design and deploy bots to automate the execution of these processes.

Different business processes may need to be executed differently. Choosing the right type of bots for the right process is the key success factor

Attended automation

Used when a bot needs to work in conjunction with a human. Runs on the user’s machine and can be triggered/controlled by a user.

Unattended automation

Used when a bot can run without human intervention. Can be run at a scheduled time or in response to an event.

Hybrid automation

Uses a combination of attended and unattended robots. Provides user support and back-end processing in a single solution.
